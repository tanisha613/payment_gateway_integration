OUR PROGRAMS

EMERGENCY MEDICAL SUPPORT

Our country is a home of the largest numbers of children population in the world. Under the Emergency Medical Programme, we support treatment to the ones who are in need of critical medical treatment. Here, we are supporting all these needy children whose parents can not afford the cost of the hospital as well as who are underprivileged. We ensure that children afflicted by Cancer or ingrained illnesses do not have to die in the dawn of their lives.

EDUCATION

We hold a high priority for quality education which is inclusive, aimed at empowering all children to become informed, competent, compassionate. Helping Hand Foundation promotes Life skills essential for living quality life and eliminating gender bias. We help female students to cope with their difficulties of day-to-day life by learning to make informed decisions, solving problems by thinking creatively and collaboratively, and building healthy relationships through effective communication.

WATER, SANITATION & HYGIENE

Ensuring Dignity and Safety by provision of Clean Toilets, Wash facilities for Girls and Boys, Educating Good Hygiene.The need of the hour is to build “Usable Efficient Toilets” covering the range of schools and villages in urban, rural and tribal regions. Apart from building toilets; the last mile - “Sustainability and maintenance” factors have to be supervised (by creating awareness programs amongst locals).



Sports For underprevileged

Helping Hand Foundation is dedicated in providing sports facilities and opportunities to rural and underprivileged children. Helping Hand Foundation aims to empower children with the values of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance while fostering their physical and mental well-being. By bridging the gap between sports and social development, Helping Hand Foundation strives to create a positive impact on the lives of these children and their communities.

GENDER EQUALITY

Poverty, Discrimination, and exploitation keep girls out of school. We work at the grassroots promoting girl child education and are a part of national flagship programme - Beti Bachao - Beti Padhao. We work at grassroots to promote Girl child education and are a part of national flagship programme BETI BACHO and BETI PADAO. Our Education programme is designed to create equal opportunities for both boys and girls thus creating a Better balance of power between genders.

ZERO HUNGER

Ensuring no Child dies of Starvation and Fighting Malnutrition. Thus Helping Hand Foundation is focused on the Child health and well being aiming at:- Reducing infant and Child Mortality by addressing malnutrition, ensuring good health. The Helping Hand Foundation organizes and encourages women to increase their knowledge on good pregnancy practices to follow so that they give birth to healthy babies and successfully nourish the growing infant



About Us

Helping Hands Foundation is an NGO that provides help to the needy children, college students and elderly in the villages. Helping Hands Foundation runs a tailoring and embroidery training center to empower women and young girls to develop. Apart from these Helping Hands Foundation actively takes part during natural calamities like Tsunami, floods etc.
